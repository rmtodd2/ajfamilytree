Narrative

Morning Tribune, Altoona, Pa., Monday, March 9, 1891

John Ellenberger, sr., born in Mifflin County, September 1, 1802, died at his home, where he resided some sixty-eight years, in Ferguson township, near Stormstown, Centre county, Monday, March 2, at 1 o'clock, aged 88 years, 6 months, and 1 day. Funeral services was held in the Lutheran church at Gatesburg, of which he was a consistant member, at 10 o'clock a.m. Wednesday, March 4, which was largely attended. Interment afterwards in the cemetery adjoining. He is survived in the immediate family by four sons and three daughters- William, Samuel, and Isaac, of Gatesburg, Centre, and John of Sinking Valley, Blair county; Mrs. Betsie Gates, Mrs. Mary Ann Rider and Mrs. Emiline Gates, all of Gatesburg, Centre county.
