Narrative

Altoona Tribune February 28 1889
Mr. John W. Ellenberger departed this life at his home near Marengo, Centre county, on Monday, aged about 60 years. Mr. Ellenberger was a good, industrious farmer, having made, by hard work, two farms. He leaves a wife, who was a Miss Ross, of Half Moon Run. He leaves the following children: James, David, George and R. N. Ellenberger, and Mrs. Barto and Mrs. Powley. He has a large number of grandchildren. Mr. Ellenberger was a member of the Methodist Episcopal Church at the Ross Church, Half Moon Charge. Rev. Noble conducted the services. The funeral was largely attended by people from that section.
