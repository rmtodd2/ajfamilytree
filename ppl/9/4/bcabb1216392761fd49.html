Jacob was born in Geonnheim, Pfalz (Germany) and came to America with his parents in 1739 when he was about 3 years old. They arrived of the ship "Robert and Alice" and settled in an area that at the time was called Lancaster Co., PA.

His father, Nicholas Ellenberger, purchased a large tract of land from the Penns in what was then Lancaster County, in the area which became North Annville Township, Lebanon County.

Jacob the farmer married Anna Huntzaker in 1772 and they had 5 boys and 5 girls named: Jacob, Christiian, Magdalena, John, Henry, Samuel, Elizabeth, Veronica, Anna and Barbara.

He served in the Revolutionary War as a private in Michael Holderbaum's Company. He is buried beside his father and wife on a private burial ground on the farm owned by Joseph Ellenberger near Annville.
