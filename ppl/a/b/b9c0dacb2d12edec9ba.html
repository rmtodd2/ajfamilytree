Narrative

Margaret Rodriquez was born on October 16, 1976 in Jacksonville, Florida the daughter of Daniel Owens and the late Jeanette Anderson Owens.

Margaret passed away at her home on June 15, 2021 surrounded by her family after battle with cervical cancer. She would like all women to be aware of cervical cancer and make sure to have routine doctor's visits to detect early if possible. She enjoyed being with her family and making jewelry.

Margaret is survived by her husband of 25 years, Daniel Lee Rodriquez; children, Thomas Lawton, Kerstan Owens, Zachary Lee Rodriquez, Dorian Lee Rodriquez, and Nicky Lee Rodriquez; father, Daniel Jerry Owens; and sister, Stephanie Bacmeister.
