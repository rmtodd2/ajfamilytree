Narrative

On Tues., June 2, at a nuptial high mass at 7 a.m., Alexander Strittmatter of Cambria county and Mary Frances Zeak of Juniata were united in matrimony by Rev. B.J. Conley at Holy Rosary Catholic church, Juniata. The attendants were C.J. Noon of Cambria county and Luella M. Zeak, sister of the bride. Immediately following came the marriage of Walter McBee of Columbus, O., and Bernadette Marie Zeak, the attendants being Mr. and Mrs. Strittmatter. Mr. and Mrs. Strittmatter have returned from eastern cities and are at the home of the bridegroom in Cambria county. Mr. and Mrs. McBee are at home to their friends at the home of the bride's parents, Mr. and Mrs. Frank Zeak, 15th ave. and 3rd st., Juniata
Altoona Mirror, Altoona, Pa., Fri., June 12, 1914
