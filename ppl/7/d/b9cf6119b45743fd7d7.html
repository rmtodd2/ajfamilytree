Narrative

Mrs. Mary Zeak. On Wednesday evening at 8:24 o'clock Mrs. Mary Zeak died at her home, No. 1603 Fourth avenue, aged about 68 years, her death being the result of an apoplectic stroke. The death was very unexpected, Mrs. Zeak having been engaged in shopping during the afternoon, and only reached her home about 4 o'clock when she was stricken down. She was a native of this State, having been born in its southern section, and was a widow. She was the mother of ten children, four sons and six daughters, five of whom are now residents of this city, as follows: Mr. John Zeak, of No. 1806 Sixth avenue, Mrs. Alice Bart, wife of John Bart, of No. 1510 Twelfth street, and Mr. David and Misses Agnes and Martha, residing at home. She is also survived by a number of grandchildren. The funeral will take place next Saturday morning, leaving the house at 8:30 and proceeds to Sacred Heart Chapel, where services will be held, after which the interment will be made in St. John's cemetery. Altoona Mirror? Dec. 26, 1890.
(Source: Blair County Genealogical Society Obituary Collection, Vol. 40, 1890.)
